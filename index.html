About

I'm Joe, the founder of Sideline Data! A few years ago I had a serious problem that I needed to solve...figuring out how much beer was left in my kegerator. After looking at cumbersome, expensive, commercial solutions I decided to tackle this problem myself with a unique solution in mind. What started out as a silly project turned into a huge development effort and a lot of fun for not just myself, but friends and family as well! That's when KegWatch was born.

As I continued working on KegWatch, I realized I could create and sell custom solutions for other problems I faced at home. Everything I build and post on this site stems from a personal need. Whether a solution didn't exist before, was too expensive, or I thought I could do it better, I am fully invested in everything I create. It has to work for me before I can share it with others. I take pride in crafting smart and fun solutions.
